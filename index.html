Racetime to Multitwitch

A site for people who prefer multitwitch over the built in spectate feature on racetime.gg

Paste the url of a racetime lobby into the input box and receive a link to a multitwitch with all the players in the lobby who have their twitch connected

Currently the conversion is only triggered by the paste button, so please use that for now
