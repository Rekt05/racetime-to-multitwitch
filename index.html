Racetime to Multitwitch

A site for people who prefer multitwitch over the built in spectate feature on racetime.gg

This site works by collecting the twitch links of users based off of a racetime.gg url and displaying them.

For the automatic mode just select the game name in the dropdown and receive the multitwitch links of all open/active races.

For the manual mode just paste the url of a racetime race into the input box and receive a multitwitch link of said race

The site requires participants to have their twitch accounts linked to their racetime profiles for it to work.

This site was developed for the hitman community, which is why that is the selected default game, but the site will remember your last selected game.

A special thanks to racetime.gg for being an amazing site.
